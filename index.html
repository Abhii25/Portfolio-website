I am a highly motivated and results-driven Cybersecurity Specialist dedicated to bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world security operations. I hold an MSc in Cybersecurity with Distinction from the University of Hertfordshire, where my academic focus solidified my deep interest in protecting digital assets.

Professional Focus & Expertise

Currently, I serve as a Cybersecurity Subject Matter Expert (SME) at PraNaga Solutions, where I leverage my expertise to provide strategic guidance on complex security challenges and compliance.

My core technical strengths:

  • Digital Forensics: Evidence Preservation, Chain of Custody, Log Analysis, Data Integrity & Hashing (SHA-1), Autopsy, Disk Imaging.
  • Cybersecurity: Penetration Testing, Risk Assessment, Threat Modeling, Security Policy Development, Network Security (Wireshark/Nmap), Cryptography.
